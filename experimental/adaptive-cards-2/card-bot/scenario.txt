A card is sent to both Teams and Outlook which has textblock 
"Hello, please write a message.", Input.Text field and an Action.Execute button "Send"

User inputs text, say "Hello World" and presses "Send"

On pressing "Send" the card is replaced with a new card which has a text block which has the user's message, 
in this case "Hello World" and an Action.Execute button "Try again"

Switching to Outlook, the card is shown synced and has the same message that user posted, in this case "Hello World"
